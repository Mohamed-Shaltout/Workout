Push Day

  • Warm-up:
    • Dynamic stretches
  • Main Workout:
    • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Flat Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Front Raises: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Lateral Raises: 5 sets of 12-15 reps
    • Tricep Kickbacks: 3 sets to failure
    • Overhead Tricep Extension: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Cool Down:
    • Static stretches

Pull Day

  • Warm-up:
    • Dynamic stretches
  • Main Workout:
    • Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Cable Reversed Grip Rows: 4 sets of 10-15 reps
    • One Hand Row: 4 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Seated Dumbbell Shrugs: 4 sets of 6-10 reps
    • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
    • Preacher Curls: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Incline Curls: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Cool Down:
    • Static stretches

Leg and Shoulder Day

  • Warm-up:
    • Light cardio (5-10 minutes)
    • Dynamic stretches
  • Main Workout:
    • Front Raises: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
    • Leg Extensions: 6 sets of 10-15 reps per leg
    • Leg Curls: 6 sets of 10-15 reps
    • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Cool Down:
    • Static stretches
